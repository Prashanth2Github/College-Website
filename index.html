Admissions

Undergraduate Admissions:

Vasavi College of Engineering admits students to various undergraduate programs through the Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture, and Medical Common Entrance Test (TS EAMCET). The selection is based on the merit rank obtained in the entrance exam.

Postgraduate Admissions:

For postgraduate admissions, candidates must have a valid Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) score or a valid Telangana State PG Engineering Common Entrance Test (TS PGECET) score. The final admission is based on merit and availability of seats in each program.

Application Process:

  • Online registration through the official VCE admissions portal.
  • Submission of required documents, including mark sheets, transfer certificates, and entrance exam scores.
  • Payment of application fees as per the guidelines.
